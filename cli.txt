   tar cvzf foo.tgz foldername
will tar the directory foldername (and its files/subdirectories) into a tar file named foo.tgz.
To see a tar file's table of contents use:

   tar tzf foo.tgz

To extract the contents of a tar file use:

    tar xvzf foo.tgz
